    profile
    Opeoluwa Shodipe is a computer geek and a research scholar with the Nigeria Institute of Medical Research. He has a B.Sc in Microbiology from Olabisi Onabanjo University (OOU), his career is devoted to research and programs to improve the health of underserved populations and eliminate health genetic disparities arising from infectious and chronic diseases, and varied impacts using molecular techniques and computational tailored interventions in underserved communities. Over this period, he has focused on pathogen-host interactions that lead to infectious diseases, drug resistance caused by mutant genes with the approach of metagenomics and bioinformatics.
